Giovanni has Undergone a Successful Surgery

Giovanni Gallo has officially returned to UNC after undergoing an extensive six-hour surgery to repair a devastating leg injury. Gallo suffered a complete reconstruction of his knee, addressing torn LCL, MCL, ACL, and meniscus ligaments, along with fractures to his femur, tibia, and kneecap. Despite the severity of the injuries, Gallo remains in good spirits and has even hinted at a potential return to the team in the future.

Kickball Season Results

The Baltimore Kickball Club fought hard through the season, finishing with a 0–2 regular season record before bowing out in the first round of the playoffs. Despite their spirit and hustle, a clear lack of kicking prowess proved to be their Achilles' heel. What they lacked in wins, they made up for in heart — and some truly unforgettable moments on the field.

BREAKING NEWS: Baltimore Basketball Falls in the First Round

After a successful regular season and going 3-0, Baltimore Basketball Club looks to make another deep run in the playoffs. However unlike the previous season, they ultimately fell in the first round and their dreams of the championship were crushed. They hope that they will be able to secure a championship in their final season.
