Charles Wilt

Blazing speed, a knockdown shooter, and a relentless rebounder for his size. "Chucky" plays like he's late for something — and you better get out of his way. If there's a loose ball, it's already his. If there's a three to be made, it's probably him too.

Bennett McNeill

Beach-born and board-crashing, Bennett is a classic paint presence. Just don’t expect him to step beyond the arc — his three-point range tops out at "paint adjacent." Like a true warrior, he plays through the pain...even when he probably shouldn't.

James Liggins

Straight from the heart of the country, JT anchors the paint with toughness, tenacity, and a love for contact...sometimes a little too much (shoutout to the IR). Despite being one play away from a WebMD sponsorship, JT is a force when healthy.

Giovanni Gallo

The heart, soul, and occasional injured reserve list of the team, Gio transitioned from head coach to player before fate (and a blown-out knee) had other plans. A proud Italian and the original architect of the team's offense. In his mind, he's still dominating the paint... in reality, he’s dominating the clipboard.

Colton Wrenn

Straight outta Roxboro — and it shows. Colton brings big country energy to the court, though his handle is... well, more John Deere than Kyrie Irving. What he lacks in dribble moves, he makes up for with pure grit and a shot that just feels like home.

Grayson McKeever

Straight out of Utah with a Pink Floyd playlist on repeat, Grayson crashes the boards and finishes through contact like every rebound owes him money. Aggressive, fearless, and somehow even more aggressive, he's the enforcer every team dreams of.

Max Gruberger

Efficiency is the name of Max’s game. A three-point marksman who rarely misses and never wastes a shot, Max makes every possession count. When he shoots, assume it's already in — it’ll save you the suspense.

Evan Kirby

If it’s beyond the arc, it’s Evan’s world — we’re just living in it. Known for his sniper-level shooting and, uh, selective dribbling, Evan spaces the floor like a seasoned veteran. Just don’t ask him to break a press.

Matthew Hargrove

Matt only has one speed — full throttle. Every minute he’s on the court, Matt plays like it’s Game 7 of the Finals. Physical, fearless, and 100% committed to doing whatever it takes to win (or at least leave a few bruises behind).
